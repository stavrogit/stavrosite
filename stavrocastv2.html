STAVROCAST

ECMWF Ensemble Forecast Uncertainty Analysis

What is this? Click to learn more

The main purpose of Stavrocast is to help users better visualize cumulative precipitation forecasts and their inherent uncertainty. The interactive map displays a 72-hour cumulative precipitation forecast for the USA. To generate a detailed forecast plot for your specific location globally, type a city name in the search box, choose your forecast duration, and click 'Fetch Forecast'. The resulting plots visualize the full range of equally probable temperature, precipitation, and wind outcomes from the latest ECMWF (European) ensemble model run.
