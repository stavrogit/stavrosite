GEFS Ensemble Forecast Uncertainty Analysis

What is this? Click to learn more

The interactive map displays a 72-hour cumulative precipitation forecast and allows users to locate specific airport codes by zooming and panning. To generate a detailed forecast plot, select a state and airport from the dropdown menus and click 'Get Forecast'. The resulting plot visualizes the complete range of probable temperature and precipitation outcomes from the latest Global Ensemble Forecast System (GEFS) model run, providing a comprehensive view of forecast uncertainty.
